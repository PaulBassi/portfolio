About Me

Hi I’m Paul, a front-end web-developer based in Toronto. Prior to my time at HackerYou, I studied Civil Engineering, where I learned the importance and qualities necessary to being a team player while also honing my strategic thinking skills.

Civil Engineering was the spark that ignited my passion for ingenuity and innovation which I was able to fully realize in my pursuit of web-development and coding.

When I’m not staring at my screen I’m still staring at my screen. With photography as my creative outlet, I often find myself looking into my viewfinder to capture landscape, candid, and the occasional hero shots of my classmates. Aside from the headshot, the images from my website are all locally sourced, straight from my camera to your screen.

My curiosity is one of my biggest drivers and naturally propels me in seeking new ways to continuously expand my horizons.

I’ve found my home amongst the machines : - )
